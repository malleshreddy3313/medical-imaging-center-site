About CMIS

Our mission is to advance the future of non-invasive diagnostics through state-of-the-art medical imaging research and education.

Vision

To be the world's leading center for translational research in medical imaging, turning scientific discovery into clinical reality faster and more efficiently than ever before. We aim to revolutionize patient care globally.

Mission

We are dedicated to fostering interdisciplinary collaboration between clinicians, scientists, and engineers to develop innovative imaging technologies and protocols, ensuring ethical, safe, and accessible diagnostics for all communities.

Leadership Message

"At CMIS, we believe that the clearest view leads to the best care. Our team is committed to pushing the boundaries of what is possible in medical visualization and analysis."

Dr. Birendra Biswal, Lead Mentor
