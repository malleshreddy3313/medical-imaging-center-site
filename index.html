Our Center Achievements

A look at our recent breakthroughs and contributions to the medical field.

Breakthrough Research Grant

Successfully led multiple SERB-funded research projects worth over ₹72 lakhs, driving impactful collaborations between academia and healthcare institutions. The research outcomes include 35+ SCIE publications (avg. IF 4.5), two international patents, and multiple MoUs with global medical organizations — positioning the team at the forefront of AI-driven medical imaging innovation.

From Research to Real-world Impact

Bridging cutting-edge AI research and clinical practice through funded projects exceeding ₹72 lakhs, pioneering patents, and strategic hospital partnerships. This ecosystem has nurtured young innovators — with students advancing to PhD and MS programs worldwide — reinforcing a legacy of global academic excellence and translational medical breakthroughs.

Empowering Next-Gen Innovators in AI & Healthcare

Cultivated a thriving research environment that secured major SERB and CSIR grants, organized national workshops, and produced globally recognized research. Mentored students who now pursue advanced studies in world-class universities, showcasing excellence in both innovation and inspiration.
