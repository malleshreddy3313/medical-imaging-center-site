Our Center Achievements

A look at our recent breakthroughs and contributions to the medical field.

Breakthrough Research Grant

Secured a multi-million dollar grant from the National Health Institute for our groundbreaking study on 7T MRI applications in neurological disorders. This funding will accelerate our research timeline significantly.

Clinical Excellence Award

Awarded the 'Clinical Excellence in Patient Care' for introducing a novel, low-dose CT scanning protocol that reduces patient radiation exposure by 40% while maintaining diagnostic image quality. This is a huge win for safety.

Academic Program Expansion

Launched a new specialized fellowship program in Computational Imaging, attracting top talent globally. We welcomed 15 new fellows from 8 countries, further establishing CMIS as a global research hub.
