Leading-Edge Research & Publications

At CMIS, our research portfolio is dedicated to revolutionizing non-invasive diagnostic imaging. We leverage advanced computational techniques, including deep learning and machine vision, to extract meaningful data from complex medical scans. Our interdisciplinary approach—fusing radiology, computer science, and molecular biology—aims to develop faster, safer, and highly accurate diagnostic tools for early disease detection, improving patient outcomes across a wide spectrum of medical fields.

Our Work: Core Projects

Project 1: 7T Neuro-Segmentation

Developing a novel, fully automated segmentation algorithm for micro-lesion identification in ultra-high field (7 Tesla) MRI scans, critical for early Alzheimer's diagnosis.

View Details

Project 2: Low-Dose Oncological CT

A clinical trial focused on using iterative reconstruction techniques to minimize radiation exposure in pediatric cancer monitoring without compromising image integrity.
